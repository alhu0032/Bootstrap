Climate Future Institue

Environment for Our Children Future

Climate Future Institute was launched on 1 March 2006. Our mission is an interdisciplinary research unit organized to conduct research and graduate education focused on variability of Earth's climate, ecosystems, and other environmental systems and on the interaction between humans and the natural environment.
